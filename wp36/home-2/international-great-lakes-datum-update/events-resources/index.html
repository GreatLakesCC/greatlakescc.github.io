The Coordinating Committee will be conducting extensive outreach as part of the IGLD (2020) update. To facilitate these efforts, a suite of downloadable resources will be generated over the next five years. These materials are free for public use, please search the content shown below and share these materials within your network! Check back periodically for new resources and/or subscribe to our IGLD (2020) Update Newsletter here for event announcements, project reports, and more.
